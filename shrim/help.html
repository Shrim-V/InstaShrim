Help/FAQ







General:

  • whatshotWhy is this website so awesome?
    It was created by an awesome genius... and Shrimad Vora.
  • settings_input_antennaWhy is this website only accessible if I have the code?
    Well, this entire website is built on a local server called Apache. This means that in order to have access to the site, one must have the SQL code and the php and html code to run it. THis is why we keep our code on Github, just incase you would like to access our site on the go or at home.
  • contact_phoneHow can I reach you guys?
    If you want to contact one of us, just hit "more" on the navbar and select the about page. Here you will find all the info you need!

Utilization:

  • searchHow do I search something?
    If you go to our main page, there is a bar on top that clearly says search. With this bar, you can search for a certain tweet or hashtag. To get to the main page, click our gorgeous logo on the navbar in the top left-hand corner of the page.
  • skip_previousHow do I get to the main page?
    That's an easy one! Just click our gorgeous logo on the navbar in the top left-hand corner of the page.
  • languageIs this website available in other languages?
    Yes! However, as we are still a developing project, we do not have a multitude of languages available for our users, however we have made every page more accessible globally by making them available in English, Spanish, and French.
  • languageHow do I change languages?
    To change the language your page is in, simply click the flag of the country whose language you want the page to be in. Again, there are only three languages available to users at the moment.
  • library_addHow do you tweet a message?
    Good question! To tweet, just type something in the bar next to the word "tweet:".
  • library_addHow do I put hashtags on tweets?
    When you tweet something, type something in the bar next to "hashtag:", and this hashtag will be associated with your tweet.
  • note_addCan I put more than one hashtag on a tweet?
    For sure! Just make sure that you separate each seperate word with a space, and our system will pick it up as separate hashtags.

Security:

  • verified_userCan other users choose the same username as me?
    Absolutely not. Usernames are unique to everybody, and we ensure that if other users are not allowed to choose usernames that are allready taken.
  • visibilityCan other people see what I tweet?
    Yes, so make sure that offensive or hurtful messages are not being sent through tweets. We here at Shrimstagram do not tolerate any acts of cyberbullying and/or discrimination.
  • lockIs my account secure?
    100%. When you set a password, our system encrypts it so that while people can have the same password, no one will be able to uncrypt and henceforth determine your password. You're safe!
